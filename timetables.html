Show Applications

To apply for a show simply contact the Station Manager at stationmanager.trinityfm@gmail.com.

In your email simply give your name, the name of your proposed show, what timeslot you'd prefer and a brief description of the content of your show.

Timeslots are given out on a first come first served basis.
